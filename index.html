About Me

I am a sales professional striving to gain more technical skills by attending UC Berkeley Extension's Coding Boot Camp. My goal is to successfully complete this course and earn his certification in full stack engineering in May of 2019. I strongly believe that gaining a base line understanding and certification in full stack engineering will exponetially augment my value to technology companies in sales, engineering, or sales/engineering hybrid roles. Upon completion of this course, I need to determine if I would like to re-enter into the workforce working for someone else, or if I would like to start something independetly on my own or with my network of peers.

For the past 8+ years, I have been employed by several startup companies in both Northern Virginia/DC and San Francisco that were successfully acquired by large tech giants (Dell and Cisco). Starting from the bottom as an SDR, I have found success across multiple business verticals (Enterprise, Mid-Market, SMB, K-12/EDU, and State and Local Gov.

In my free time I enjoy playing sports such as basketball and snowboarding, reading, and traveling. Originally from Philadelphia, I am also a big fan of the Philadelphia Eagles, Phillies, and 76ers.
